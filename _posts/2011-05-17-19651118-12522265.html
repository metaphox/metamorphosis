--- title: 「fair enough」这个短语有合适的对应中文说法吗？ layout: post --- 部分情况下可以用“好吧”替代：

例：
Person 1: Why did you go to that lame school? ——咋上那个破学校去了？
Person 2: Because they gave me a full ride. ——全奖。
Person 1: Fair enough. ——好吧。

又：
I think the Red Sox will win the World Series this year. ——今年红袜会得冠哟。
Fair enough.——好吧。

或：
1. "I hate myspace"
"Fair enough" ——好吧。
2. "The over use of the words fair and enough are causing our generation to be poor communicators"
"Fair" ——哦。
3. "I shot JR"
"Fair play to you man"——哼哼屌啊你。
